Co-teaching

發表於 2024-12-07 更新於 2024-12-09 分類於 Paper 文章字數： 4.4k 所需閱讀時間 ≈ 8 分鐘

Abstract

  1. This paper proposed paradigm called Co-teaching, which trains deep neural networks robustly under noisy supervision

  2. The Key is to maintain two networks simultaneously, and cross-trains on instances screened by the “small loss” criteria.

    • Author trains two deep neural networks simultaneously, and let them teach each other given every mini-batch:
      • Each network feeds forward all data and selects some data of possibly clean labels.
      • Two networks communicate with each other what data in this mini-batch should be used for training.
      • Each network back propagates the data selected by its peer network and updates itself.
    • Investigate the theoretical guarantees for Co-teaching

  3. Experiment The papaer conducts MNIST, CIFAR-10 and CIFAR-100 on noisy labels. The results is that co-teaching is much superior to the state-of-the-art methods in the robustness of trained deep models.
